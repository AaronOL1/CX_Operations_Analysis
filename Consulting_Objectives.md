# 🎯 Analytical Framework & Consulting Objectives

To ensure this Customer Experience (CX) analysis drives actionable business decisions, the following strategic objectives have been established prior to data manipulation:

### 1. The Bottleneck Impact
* **Define Wait-Time Tolerances:** Determine the exact minute-mark threshold where operational delays (Departure/Arrival) cause a critical drop in customer satisfaction.
* **Segment Delay Impact:** Quantify the difference in delay tolerance between Loyal and Disloyal customer segments to prioritize operational recovery efforts.

### 2. The Microjourney Assessment
* **Isolate Key Drivers:** Identify the top 3 service touchpoints (e.g., Online booking, In-flight wifi, Leg room) that have the strongest correlation with a positive NPS/Satisfaction rating.
* **Map Phase Friction:** Evaluate performance across the customer journey to pinpoint whether the primary loss of satisfaction occurs during the "Planning/Booking" phase or the "Service Delivery" phase.

### 3. Retention Strategy
* **Correlate Service Quality with Loyalty:** Model the relationship between average touchpoint scores and customer retention to develop a proactive strategy for at-risk accounts.
